# My Rostov (Мой Ростов)

**My Rostov** is a smart city companion app designed for residents and visitors of Rostov-on-Don.

The application combines essential urban services, city navigation, and local information
into a single, easy-to-use mobile experience.

## Features

- **User profile with rating and achievements**  
  Earn points for activity, level up, unlock titles, and stay visible within the city community.

- **Intelligent city services**  
  From ordering a taxi or calling a specialist to planning walks and routes around the city.

- **Smart city map**  
  Cafes, parks, gas stations, parking areas, shops, and other important locations with filters,
  recommendations, and up-to-date information.

- **City news and events**  
  Verified local news, urgent updates, and cultural announcements in one place.

- **Fast and simple authorization**  
  Quick registration with secure access to all features.
